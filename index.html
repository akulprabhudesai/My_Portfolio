Healthcare Management System

Idea of Healthcare Management System was originated from world's biggest pandamic ever, Covid-19, where patients and health providers were struggling to come in contact with each other for medication all across the globe. So, there was a need of a medium which will allow patients and doctors to connect with each other without any physical meeting and our Healthcare Management System provides exactly the same via tele conferencing. This SaaS product allows doctors to examine patients, upload prescriptions and maintain each patient's file via Tele Conferencing. All you need is internet connection and a laptop, and you will be able to connect with your doctor with ease.
